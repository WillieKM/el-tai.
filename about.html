About El Tai Transport Services

El Tai Transport Services provides reliable and compassionate non-emergency medical transportation throughout Snohomish County, King County, and Pierce County Washington.

Our wheelchair accessible vehicles allow passengers with mobility challenges to travel safely to medical appointments including:

  • Dialysis appointments
  • Hospital visits
  • Rehabilitation treatments
  • Routine medical checkups

Our mission is to provide safe, comfortable, and dependable transportation for patients who need assistance traveling to and from healthcare facilities.
